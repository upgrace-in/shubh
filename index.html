ABOUT US


LEADING EVENT MANAGEMENT COMPANY IN INDIA

Taking that idea from concept to reality requires a unique combination: creativity, a desire for excellence & immaculate organizational skills.

The success of shubhkamanaye is a result of the team working tirelessly to ensure that every component of the event unravels exactly as planned. A factor that unites each member of the team is a ‘passion for experience’ - a steadfast commitment to ensuring that every event is an experience, a memory that will stay in the mind of those present long after they have left the venue.

shubhkamanaye is listed on top event management companies in India. shubhkamanaye was founded in 2017; the founding team noticed that there was a clear gap in the industry for a professional and reliable innovation-driven event management company. Wedding Events, Theme Parties, Birthday Party, Anniversary Celebrations, Corporate Events, Award Nights, Exhibitions, Annual Days, etc. are some of our core services.

Anita Makharia, the founder of shubhkamanaye, is a passionate and dedicated event planner and currently serves as the Treasurer of Karnataka Event Management Association (KEMA) with 80+ renowned Event Management Companies. She also serves as the Honorable member in the Expert Panel with the most esteemed Indira Gandhi National Open University - IGNOU Swayam Courses on Event Management. She is currently also pursuing Post-Graduate Certificate in Sales and Marketing Communication by MICA. She is the Head of Strategy and Planning with Adtree and was instrumental towards making shubhkamanaye a pan India company. Having worked with corporate giant s like Byju’s, Godrej Properties, Royal Oak, Shriram Properties, Sudarshan Silks, etc., she brings with her rich and varied experience in corporate, weddings and all kinds of events.
